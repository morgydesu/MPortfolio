01. The Challenge

Telstra’s marketing teams were manually managing campaign assets across multiple disconnected platforms. This "swivel-chair" process led to data entry errors, delayed launches, and a lack of transparency for stakeholders.

The Goal: To centralize campaign management and automate the hand-off between marketing strategy and technical distribution.

02. Product Strategy

Stakeholder Alignment

Conducted user interviews with 12 marketing leads to map the current "pain-points" and identify requirements for the MVP.

Prioritization

Used the RICE framework to prioritize features, focusing on automated notifications first to solve the transparency gap.

03. The Implementation

I led the development of a custom orchestration engine using Power Apps and Power Automate. This solution served as a single source of truth, connecting marketing inputs directly to downstream execution teams.
