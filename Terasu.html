Terasu: A solution to job security

A hybrid digital and physical product ecosystem designed to bridge the 'confidence gap' for Australian career starters.

Role

Product Lead

Duration

12 Weeks

Impact

SDG 8.5 Alignment

Key Focus

Scalability & UX

01. The Problem Landscape

Australia is facing a rising youth unemployment rate of 9.5%. Our research identified a critical "skills mismatch": while jobs exist, career starters lack the peer-validated soft skills required to secure them.

The Goal: To build a scalable product that facilitates self-reflection and professional mentorship, moving users from "unqualified" to "career-ready."

02. Product Strategy

User Research

Conducted 16 interviews and analyzed 36 survey responses, finding that while 61% of starters value soft skills, 41% lack confidence in them.

Iterative Design

Evolved from a "buddy system" to a dual-app mentorship model (Mentees/Mentors) based on three rounds of user testing.

03. The Implementation

We developed a "Wall of Motivation" physical installation for campuses paired with a digital app. This unified experience uses a mirror to attract users, themed reflection prompts, and a LinkedIn-integrated mentorship system.

05. Key Takeaways

Ruthless Prioritization

To meet the 12-week MVP deadline, I prioritized the mentorship matching algorithm and QR-integration over a social "feed" feature. This ensured we delivered the core value proposition (skill validation) first.

Stakeholder Alignment

Managed the pivot from a peer-to-peer buddy system to a professional mentor model by aligning user research data with SDG 8.5 goals, ensuring the product remained viable for university partnerships.

Omni-channel Strategy

Recognized that digital fatigue is high among students. Using a physical "Wall of Motivation" acted as a high-visibility entry point, driving user growth by leveraging existing high-traffic campus locations instead of relying on expensive digital ads.

Technical Feasibility

Balanced the 3D aesthetic of the "Wall" with the practical constraints of web-based QR scanning to ensure a frictionless "Scan-to-App" user journey for first-time visitors.
